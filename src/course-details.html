About course

The guidelines of various agencies and associations regarding cardiopulmonary resuscitation are periodically updated. And if everything is relatively stable with basic CPR, in matters of extended CPR everything is much more complicated: rescue chains, biosafety issues, work in a heterogeneous medical environment in different countries in terms of material and technical security - things that make the ordinary doctor and his management spin their heads.

Our platform offers a structured 3-day extended CPR course from Andrey Varvinsky based on the recommendations of the European Resuscitation Council 2021. The problems under consideration include not only the features of directly extended CPR, but also aspects of planning, ethics, and postresuscitation care for children and adults.

Schedule

  1. Rescue services and epidemiology.

    Various modern methods of CPR will be described using telephone instructions from dispatchers, using drones, etc. We will announce the frequency of out-of-hospital and nosocomial acute circulatory arrest (OOC) and the outcomes of CPR.

  2. A universal ABCDE approach to assessing the patient's condition.

    A systematic approach to assessing the patient's condition and severity assessment scale will be proposed.

  3. Coronary and non-coronary causes of OOC.

    Let us examine the causes of OOK and the algorithm of actions in acute coronary syndrome.

  4. Basic CPR.

    OOK confirmation algorithm, sequence of actions for OOK.

  5. Universal CPR algorithm.

    The quintessence of the entire course with an analysis of the two main types of OOK, further actions, the search for possible causes of OOK and their elimination.
